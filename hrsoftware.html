Technology has changed Human Resourse Management System. Through WYS HRMS, you can map your organization human resource model to reflect your own enterprise structures and policies. WYS HRMS can be customized as per your organization's need. This Online HRIS model lets you record the Attendance data, Absentee & Leave Management, personal, work, and pay information for all the employees. Cloud based HRMS module is both flexible and adaptable. It is flexible, so that you can reflect the needs of different companies, or different groups within the same company. It is adaptable, so that you can easily change the basic model as your enterprise changes.
